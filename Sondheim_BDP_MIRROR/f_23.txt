Alan Anguish shakily walks down the corridor of Media Moo looking for an 

angry fix. There's nothing there, contraptions frozen in an icy maw. 

Veronica refuses him her favors once again, Dewey speaks and shuts the 

hell up. He gets lost in the origin of numbers, pulls out, shrieks back 

to planet earth. All the words are his; the injections don't take any 

more, though - what can you do with a dismembered body? A. A. remembers 

Archie, splayed open, hunched against command line k:22> and turns on for 

abuse. Archie's busy. Archie's not _that_ busy. Alan splays for Veronica, 

for no one else. Archie turns the lever. Alan searches for abuse:

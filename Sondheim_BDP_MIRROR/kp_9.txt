


Note on Relay


You can't mail relay any more. You can't relay internally, you can't use
bang paths (hop paths), you can't telnet <host> 25 to increasing numbers
of hosts. What does this mean? That hosts are fortifying themselves, of
necessity, behind firewalls - that the porous internet is becoming the
defended Internet. Of course the r- (remote) commands have been, by and
large, disabled, although I still have rlogin running between panix and
gol. Not to mention that very few have used or heard of finger, finger
